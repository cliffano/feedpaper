Why FeedTouch?

There are still so many web sites which are not optimized for mobile devices. They are painful to read, and you have to keep zooming in and out, scrolling past the ads, and flipping through fragments of an article.

FeedTouch provides you with an easy way to discover the content of a web site via its Atom/RSS feed, and to read the web pages with fonts and styles that are easy on the eyes, all from the comfort of your phone.

Usage

Simply add ft.prn.la in front of a web site URL.
For example ft.prn.la/wired.com

Kudos

FeedTouch is mashed up with Google Feed and ViewText APIs.
