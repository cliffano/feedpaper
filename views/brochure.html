Why FeedTouch?

There are still so many websites which are not optimized for mobile devices. They are painful to read, you keep having to zoom in and out, scroll past the ads, and flip through fragments of an article.

FeedTouch provides you with an easy way to discover the content of those websites via their Atom/RSS feed, and to read the pages using fonts and styles that are easy on the eyes, all from the comfort of your phone.

Usage

Simply add ft.t00.me in front of a website URL.
For example ft.prn.la/wired.com

Kudos

FeedTouch is mashed up with Google Feed and ViewText.
