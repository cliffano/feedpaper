Why?

FeedTouch makes Atom/RSS feed discovery real easy from your mobile phone, it also improves the readability of the feed content.

Usage

Simply add ft.t00.me in front of a website address, e.g. ft.t00.me/wired.com, then bookmark the page or add it to the home screen.

Or use this bookmarklet: FeedTouch me.

Share

Hat Tip

FeedTouch is a mashup of Google Feed + Readability, sprinkled with Node.js and jQuery Mobile.
