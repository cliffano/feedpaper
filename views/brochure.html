Why FeedTouch?

There are so many web sites not optimized for mobile devices. You often waste your time trying to read a web page, zooming in and out, scrolling past the ads, and flipping through fragments of an article.

FeedTouch makes it easy to discover the content of a web site via its Atom/RSS feeds, and to read the web pages conveniently[1] on your mobile device.

[1] Thanks to ViewText.

Usage

Simply add ft.prn.la in front of a web site URL.
For example: ft.prn.la/cnn.com
